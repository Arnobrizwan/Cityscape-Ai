Cityscape-Ai is an intelligent urban management system designed to optimize traffic, improve infrastructure resilience, and support sustainable city planning in Kuala Lumpur. It uses Graph Neural Networks (GNN) for spatial graph modeling and Reinforcement Learning (RL) for dynamic decision-making such as traffic light control and routing strategies. The system ingests geospatial data from OpenStreetMap and real-time traffic data sources. Its core components include simulation, analytics, and a conversational chatbot powered by a fine-tuned SentenceTransformer model and Gemini API.
